Project Overview

The project consists of a reinforced concrete G+3 residential building designed within an urban site context. The structure includes ground floor parking, repetitive residential floors, balconies, and a central staircase core. The digital strategy supports coordinated design, construction efficiency, and lifecycle optimisation.

Building Information Modelling (BIM)

A comprehensive BIM model was developed to coordinate architectural and structural components. The model enables clash detection, 4D scheduling simulation, and 5D cost estimation, ensuring design accuracy and efficient project delivery.

Building Sections

Sectional views demonstrate vertical circulation, floor levels, structural configuration, and spatial organisation across the building. These coordinated sections support structural validation and construction planning.

Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT technologies are proposed for structural health monitoring, environmental sensing, occupancy tracking, and energy management. Real-time data collection enhances safety, operational efficiency, and proactive maintenance strategies.

Artificial Intelligence Applications

Artificial intelligence supports predictive maintenance, risk assessment, and energy optimisation through advanced data analysis. Machine learning models enhance decision-making accuracy throughout the building lifecycle.

Digital Twin and Lifecycle Management

The digital twin integrates BIM and IoT systems to create a dynamic virtual representation of the physical building. Continuous data synchronisation enables performance monitoring and lifecycle optimisation.

Big Data Analytics

Big data analytics consolidates BIM outputs, sensor data, and AI predictions into structured dashboards. This enhances evidence-based decision-making and continuous performance improvement.

Integrated Implementation Strategy

A phased implementation approach ensures systematic deployment of BIM, IoT, AI, digital twin, and analytics technologies. This strategy balances investment, productivity improvements, and long-term operational benefits.
