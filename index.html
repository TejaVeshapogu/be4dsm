Project Overview

The project consists of a reinforced concrete G+3 residential building designed within an urban site context.

Building Information Modelling (BIM)

A comprehensive BIM model was developed to coordinate architectural and structural components.

Internet of Things (IoT)

IoT technologies are proposed for structural health monitoring and environmental sensing.

Artificial Intelligence Applications

Artificial intelligence supports predictive maintenance and risk assessment.

Digital Twin and Lifecycle Management

The digital twin integrates BIM and IoT systems to create a dynamic virtual representation.

Big Data Analytics

Big data analytics consolidates BIM outputs and sensor data into dashboards.

Integrated Implementation Strategy

A phased implementation approach ensures systematic deployment of technologies.
